Our Coffee

Built on 100% specialty Arabica. Featuring Ratnagiri Estate beans — known for clean, balanced cups with fruit-forward and chocolate notes. Double washed processing brings clarity and precision to every extraction.

Double Washed Process

Our Croissants

Croissants aren’t just pastries here — they’re a ritual. Made in-house at our Baking Studio - The Layers, rolled and baked by us — folded with care, baked golden. Tradition meeting obsession.

Baked at Layers.

Our Hot Chocolate

More than comfort — it’s craft. Our hot chocolate is made with single-origin cacao, sourced from farms in Kanjirapilly, Kottayam, Kerala — whisked into rich, velvety cups that carry the story of the farms they come from. Pure, deep, and unforgettable.
