Crafted With Passion

From buttery croissants to rich hot chocolate, every item is made to bring people closer. A menu that’s simple, but memorable.

A Space for Connection

In a world that moves fast, we create pauses. Every croissant tells a story. Every cup holds a conversation. Every silence feels warm. This is more than coffee—it’s 90Million.

Our Coffee

Espresso here is pure — built on 100% Arabica beans from Lavazza, Italy. For manual brews, we go local: single-origin beans from India’s best estates, making every cup a quiet mission to support farmers and preserve India’s coffee heritage.

Our Hot Chocolate

More than comfort — it’s craft. Our hot chocolate is made with single-origin cacao, whisked into rich, velvety cups that carry the story of the farms they come from. It’s indulgence done right — pure, deep, and unforgettable.

Our Croissants

Not frozen. Not yesterday. Our croissants are rolled, layered, and baked fresh at our central kitchen every single day. Buttery, flaky, honeycombed perfection — the way they’re meant to be.

Sustainability

We don’t “minimize” plastic. We don’t use it — period. From cornstarch straws to paper-first packaging, sustainability is baked into every detail — just like butter in our croissants. Cafés should nourish, not pollute.

The 90Million Way

We’re not chasing trends. We’re building a space that lasts. A place where you can drink better, eat better, live better. A space for conversations, pauses, and possibilities. This is what 90Million is about — timeless, thoughtful, and true.
